About

"I believe every pet deserves to feel safe, happy, and loved — especially when their owner is away."

My name is Elena, and I've always been passionate about animals and the unique personalities that make each one so special. A few years ago I became the happy parent of Arya, my cat, and caring for her made me realise how much I love truly understanding an animal's individual needs, routines, and preferences.

I currently work a part-time morning job, which gives me great flexibility in the afternoons, evenings, and weekends — so I can dedicate real time to every pet I care for, and adjust my schedule to fit their routine when needed.

Every visit comes with photos and videos so you're never left wondering. If something seems off — appetite, behaviour, energy — you hear about it the same day, not when you get home.
